Meet The Team

The Nafel team consists of highly skilled professionals, engineers, designers, and industry experts who are passionate about delivering excellence in every project. Our diverse team brings together decades of combined experience across engineering, construction, technology, and creative industries.

We believe that our people are our greatest asset. Each team member is carefully selected for their expertise, dedication, and commitment to our core values of excellence, integrity, innovation, and collaboration. Together, we work as a unified force to deliver outstanding results for our clients.

Hisham Mahdi

Hisham started his career in oil fields as Wireline Instrument Field Engineer in China and Oman (2010-2012), then transitioned to construction engineering in Saudi Arabia building factories and warehouses in King Abdullah Economic City.

He managed an international construction company's branch in UAE & Saudi Arabia before co-founding Nafel as an electromechanical company.

Education:

  • Bachelors of Electrical Engineering - Queensland University of Technology (2010)
